Step 3: Collecting your print

1. Logging

Image placeholder.

It is mandatory to log all prints!

Log your filament usage and total printing time taken in the provided log book.

2. Removing the print

Image placeholder

Use the provided scraper to carefully remove the print from the build plate.

If your model is stuck onto the plate, insert the sharp tip of the scraper under the model to pry it off.

Be careful to not scratch the sticky tape off the build plate.

3. You're done!

Please clean up after yourself and put back all the tools neatly for the next user.
