Specifications

Mankati Fullscale XT Plus

Build Volume: 260mm × 260mm × 300mm (height)
Do not exceed 90% of the build plate size.

Print Resolution:
Nozzle: 0.4mm diameter
XY Placement Accuracy: 0.1mm
Min. Z Layer Height: 0.05mm

Absolute Maximum Temperature:
Extruder: 300°C
Build Plate: 110°C
Under normal circumstances, you should never reach these temperatures!

Filament:
You are only allowed to use 3mm PLA filament provided by the Fab Lab.

Other Information:
Compatible Slicers: MankatiUM(Recommended), Cura (requires expert settings)
