Step 2: Printing

1. Load .gcode file into SD Card

Use MankatiUM to export the your model (.stl) into the .gcode format. Further instructions can be found here.

2. Load SD Card into printer

The printed side of the card should be facing up, and will slot into place. Do not force the SD card in!

3. Remove excess filament

Using a pair of scissors, carefully snip/tug away excess filament on the extruder nozzle. Be careful not to scratch the nozzle.

Now would also be a good time to remove any leftover debris from the printer.

4. Start printing

Menu Sequence: ENTER → Print from SD Card → file.gcode

Once the extruder head and bed is hot enough, the printing will begin.

Do watch your print for the first one or two layer for problems!
If you encounter any problems, please review the troubleshooting procedures or call Fab Lab staff for help.
