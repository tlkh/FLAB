Step 1: Preparation

1. Auto Home

Menu Sequence: ENTER → Prepare → Auto home
Push the control knob in to give the ENTER command.

Press ENTER.

The build plate will begin to move up towards the extruder. It will stop when it reaches the extruder.

This step resets the Z = 0.0 mm point.

2. Loading Filament

Preheat the extruder.

Menu Sequence: ENTER → Prepare → Preheat PLA → Preheat PLA 1

Press ENTER.

The extruder will begin to heat up to the target temperature from the current temperature. These are marked in the picture above.

While it is heating, move the build plate down to Z = 30.0 mm or more. Do not turn the knob too far ahead as the printer will follow through with the command even if it is reversed!

Menu Sequence: ENTER → Prepare → Move axis → 1mm → Move Z

Press ENTER and turn the control knob clockwise.

Back out into the info screen. Once the target temperature is reached, we can begin to extrude some filament.

Menu Sequence: ENTER → Prepare → Move axis → 1mm → Move Extruder

Press ENTER and turn the control knob clockwise.

If the extruder head is hot enough and filament is loaded properly, filament will begin to flow out of the extruder head. If this is not the case, contact Fab Lab staff for help.
