Specifications

Edison+ Single

Build Volume: 225mm × 145mm × 200mm (height)
Try not to exceed 90% of the build plate size.

Nozzle: 0.4mm diameter
Heated Bed: None

Filament:
You are only allowed to use 1.75mm PLA filament provided by the Fab Lab.

Other Information:
Compatible Slicers: CreatorK 9.0
