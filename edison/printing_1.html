Before printing, you should have already:

  1. Prepared your .x3g file
  2. Estimated your printing time
  3. Check if the printer has sufficient filament, and that the filament is not entangled
  4. Log your print inside the log book

Navigating the Menu:

Use the direction pad to navigate the menu.
Press the left arrow to go to the previous screen.

Step 1: Preparation

1. Preheat

Menu Sequence: Down → Preheat →

Press

Printer will begin to heat extruder head to 230°C (default setting). Do not touch the extruder head with your bare hands.
Press the left arrow to exit to the main screen.

2. Loading Filament

Menu Sequence: Utilities → Filament Loading

Select Load and press

When the extruder head is hot enough, you will hear a tone. Filament will begin to flow out of the extruder head.
Once the flow is constant, press (not the left arrow!) to stop the loading procedure.
