Step 1: Preparation

1. Preheat Filament

Image placeholder.

Menu Sequence: → Preheat

Printer will begin to heat extruder head to 230°C (default setting). Do not touch the extruder head with your bare hands.

Press the left arrow to exit.

2. Filament Loading

Image placeholder

Menu Sequence: → Utilities → Loading/Unloading → Load Filament

Image placeholder

Once the extruder head is hot enough, filament will begin to flow out of the extruder head. You will hear a tone.

Once the flow is steady, press (not the left arrow!) to stop the loading procedure.
