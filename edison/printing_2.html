Step 2: Printing

1. Load .x3g file into SD Card

Use CreatorK to export the your model (.stl) into the .x3g format, not .gcode! Further instructions can be found here.

2. Load SD Card into printer

Placeholder image

The printed side of the card should be facing away from you, and will click into place. Do not force the SD card in!

3. Remove excess filament

Using a scissors, carefully snip/tug away excess filament on the extruder nozzle. Be careful not to scratch the nozzle.

Now would also be a good time to remove any leftover debris from the printer.

4. Start printing

Image placeholder

Menu Sequence: → Print from SD Card → file.x3g

Once the extruder head is hot enough, the printing will begin.

Do watch your print for the first one or two layer for problems! If you encounter any problems, please review the troubleshooting procedures or call Fab Lab staff for help.
